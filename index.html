About

Welcome to Katie’s cookbook. This site is about cooking, and about recipes. I call it my cookbook because I share my favorite recipes with you. Some recipes are known worldwide, some of them are just my family recipes and some of them can be only experiments. I’ve tried to create easy and simple categories so you can find exactly what you are looking for. Try it yourself!

Why

The reason for creating this webpage is simpler than you might think. First, it was my school project. However, I have chosen this topic on purpose. I always struggled with planning my meals and eventually cooking them. Sometimes all I needed was just an inspiration. So, I thought maybe someone is going through the same problem, and I’ve decided that I want to help those people. I want to create a nice community of people who like cooking and who are looking for inspiration or help with choosing what to cook. And I am just a beginner in cooking which means that everything here is beginner friendly and together we can gain more experience in this skill. So, if you are also this kind of a person, come and join us!
