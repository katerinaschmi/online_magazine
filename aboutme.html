About me

Hello, my name is Katerina Schmidtova. People usually call me just Kate or Katie. I’m 19 years old girl from the Czech Republic. I’m very friendly and open-minded. I like to inspire people and try out new things as well. I’m actually not a big cook, yet, so this is all new and challenging for me as well. Thanks for looking through my cookbook and you can get to know a little bit more about me lower. Love you!
